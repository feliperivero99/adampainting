Northern Michigan interior painting specialists

At Adams Custom Painting LLC , our interior painters specialize in residential & commercial painting, staining, wall repair and wallpaper removal. Preparation is the key for all painting projects. Our painters will cover and protect the flooring, window treatments, kitchen cabinets, etc., move and cover all furniture and remove all cover plates. We work with you to decide what types of paints and colors meet your design needs. Additionally, after each painting project is completed, we clean thoroughly and place everything back in its original location.

Interior Painting Process

This process starts with the estimate and is continued with our professional painting staff that will be working with you on your project. When Adams Custom Painting, LLC paints your interior, you can expect the following:

  • An arranged time of work completion that is mutually agreed upon.
  • Professional painters that take pride in their work.
  • Complete paint preparation before the painting begins.
  • Job site will be kept clean and organized while the work is in progress.
  • Colors to match your requirements.
  • Two coat application for all color changes.
  • Premium grade products

The Role Of Color

Provided your color co-ordinates with your furnishings, painting your walls can have a positive impact on a room. Shades of white, beige, and gray provide a neutral setting that will not compete with your furniture. If you are looking for a more dramatic change, why not try a color that is complimentary to your accessories

Complimentary colors are opposite to one another on the color wheel (i.e. blue and orange, yellow and purple, red and green). A color scheme using the primary colors, red, yellow, and blue is another option and can give your room a strong, solid feeling. At Adams Custom Painting, LLC, we have online color tools that can help you find the right color for your next painting project.

Our Company

My name is Chris Adams and I am the owner of Adams Custom Painting LLC and Deck Doctors. I attended Central Michigan University and was an active member in Delta Sigma Phi fraternity. I moved to Traverse City in 1993 right out of college and started a full service pressure washing company with a fraternity brother until 1999. In 2000, I opened Adams Custom Painting LLC and Deck Doctors.

Adams Custom Painting LLC and Deck Doctors are established companies that have been serving northern Michigan for over 12 years. We are focused on building long lasting customer relationships by offering the most professional painting services in the area. Our customers choose us for these reasons:

  • Positive reputation in the community
  • Clean, responsible and thoughtful employees
  • Owner operated company
  • Fully insured
  • Many satisfied customers with excellent references

Ask us about Virtual Painting for all complete interior and exterior painting jobs! Through color imaging, we can show our customers what their new colors will look like on their own walls, before we paint. This makes the decision of selecting a new color scheme for your home a less stressful experience. We also will work closely with you to pick out colors and display color swatches either on samples or on your walls. We look forward to working with you on all of your painting projects
